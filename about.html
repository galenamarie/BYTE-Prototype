>BYTE<

A small cluster of data can add up to a very complete picture of who we are and what we do.
As younger generations are born into an increasingly online/digital world,
the general population is becoming more and more comfortable with allowing their data to be
collected for the sake of convinience and entertainment.
BYTE seeks to bring awareness to how our personal data can be collected to provide a
startlingly complete image of our population. As the user engages in a very simple
4-step process, they traverse the gap between individuality and anonymity amongst
hundreds of other data peices. Spectators do not have to participate, though anyone can
become part of the peice if they willingly give permission - just as we freely (and perhaps unknowingly)
give permission for information about us to be collected every day.
